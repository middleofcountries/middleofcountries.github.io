2. Description of Service

Middle of Countries provides a publicly accessible database of geographic centroids (geographic centers) for sovereign nations worldwide. The data is calculated using the Area-Weighted Centroid algorithm on the WGS84 ellipsoid and is intended for educational, research, and informational purposes.
