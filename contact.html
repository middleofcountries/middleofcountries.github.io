Commercial Licensing

Our geographic centroid data is freely available for educational and non-commercial use. For commercial applications, a licensing agreement is required.

Please contact us with details about your intended use case, expected data volume, and commercial context.

middleofcountries@gmail.com

Data Corrections

If you believe a geographic center coordinate is inaccurate, please provide the country name, the coordinates you believe are correct, and your source reference.

All correction requests are reviewed by our geodesy team within 5–10 business days.
